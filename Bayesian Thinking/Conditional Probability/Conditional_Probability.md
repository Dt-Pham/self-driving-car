# Conditional Probability

## Learning Objective

#### Medical Example

P(cancer) = 0.1

P($\neg$cancer) = 0.9

P(positive | cancer) = 0.9

P(negative | cancer) = 0.1

P(positive | $\neg$cancer) = 0.2

P(negative | $\neg$cancer) = 0.8
